List of colleges and universities in Hawaii

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

This is a list of colleges and universities in Hawaii. This list also includes other accredited educational institutions providing higher education, meaning tertiary, quaternary, and, in some cases, post-secondary education.

Public institutions[edit]

Community colleges[edit]

  • Hawaii Community College, Hilo
  • Honolulu Community College, Honolulu
  • Kapiolani Community College, Honolulu
  • Kauai Community College, Līhu'e
  • Leeward Community College, Pearl City
  • Windward Community College, Kāne'ohe

Public universities[edit]

  • University of Hawaii at Hilo, doctoral university in Hilo
  • University of Hawaii at Manoa, doctoral university in Honolulu
  • University of Hawaii-West Oahu, baccalaureate university in Kapolei
  • University of Hawaii Maui College, baccalaureate university in Kahului, Hawaii

Private, non-profit institutions[edit]

  • Brigham Young University Hawaii, baccalaureate university affiliated with the LDS Church in Lā'ie
  • Chaminade University of Honolulu, master's university affiliated with the Roman Catholic Church in Honolulu
  • Hawaii Pacific University, nonsectarian undergraduate, graduate, and research university in Honolulu
  • Hawaii Tokai International College, nonsectarian associate's college in Kapolei
  • Remington College, Honolulu

For-profit institutions[edit]

  • Argosy University, Honolulu
  • University of Phoenix, Honolulu

Special-focus institutions[edit]

  • Institute for Clinical Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine, Honolulu
  • World Medicine Institute, Honolulu
