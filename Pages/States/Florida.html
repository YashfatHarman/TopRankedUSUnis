Seal of the State of Florida

The following is a list of colleges and universities in the U.S. state of Florida. Many of these schools have multiple campuses. In such cases, only the location of the main campus in Florida is specified. Most public institutions and traditional private institutions in Florida are accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools.
