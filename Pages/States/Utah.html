Public[edit]

Research universities[edit]

  • University of Utah, Salt Lake City
  • Utah State University, Logan
    • USU-Brigham City, Brigham City
    • USU-Tremonton, Tremonton, Utah
    • USU-Tooele, Tooele
    • USU-Uintah Basin, Vernal and Roosevelt
    • USU-CEU, Price and Blanding

Master's universities[edit]

  • Southern Utah University, Cedar City
  • Utah Valley University, Orem
  • Weber State University, Ogden
  • Dixie State University (prospective)

Four-year[edit]

  • Dixie State University, St. George, Utah

Two-year[edit]

Associate's colleges[edit]

  • Snow College, Ephraim and Richfield
  • Salt Lake Community College, various locations in Salt Lake County

Technical colleges[edit]

Regional Campuses of the Utah College of Applied Technology

  • Bridgerland Applied Technology College, Logan
  • Dixie Applied Technology College, St. George
  • Mountainland Applied Technology College, Orem
  • Ogden-Weber Applied Technology College, Ogden
  • Tooele Applied Technology College, Tooele
  • Southwest Applied Technology College, Cedar City
  • Uintah Basin Applied Technology College, Roosevelt
  • Davis Applied Technology College, Kaysville

Private nonprofit[edit]

Research universities[edit]

  • Brigham Young University, Provo

Four-year[edit]

  • Westminster College, master's liberal arts institution in Salt Lake City
  • Western Governors University, nonprofit distance learning institution headquartered in Salt Lake City

Two-year[edit]

  • LDS Business College, Salt Lake City

Other schools[edit]

  • Columbia College Utah, regional campus in Salt Lake City of Columbia College, a Columbia, Missouri-based school.
  • George Wythe University, unaccredited liberal arts school
  • Roseman University of Health Sciences, regional campus in South Jordan of a Henderson, Nevada-based school.
  • Stevens-Henager College, Ogden, Murray, Orem, St. George, Logan, Online (Independence University)[1]
  • Webster University Utah, regional campus in Murray of Webster University, a St. Louis-based school.

For-profit[edit]

  • Fortis College, Salt Lake City
  • Eagle Gate College, Murray, Layton, Salt Lake City
  • Neumont University, South Jordan
  • Provo College, Provo
  • Rocky Mountain University of Health Professions, Provo
  • University of Phoenix, Salt Lake City
  • Broadview University, Layton, West Jordan, Orem, Salt Lake City
  • ITT Technical Institute, Murray
  • Ameritech College[1], Draper, Provo
  • Vista College Online, Clearfield
